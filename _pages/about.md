Welcome to my personal academic website.

I am **Dr. Amr Hamada**, a solar physicist at the **National Solar Observatory (NSO/NISP)** in Boulder, Colorado, and affiliated with the **University of Colorado Boulder**.  
My research focuses on:

- Helioseismology  
- Far–side solar imaging  
- Machine learning applications in solar physics  
- Solar Orbiter PHI & GONG far-side magnetic flux reconstruction  
- Space weather forecasting and global magnetic field mapping  

This site includes my publications, research projects, talks, teaching materials, and ongoing work in solar physics and machine-learning–driven far-side imaging.
